## Send

- Send mail: `Ctrl-Enter`

## Create

- New email: `Ctrl-Shift-M`

## Navigate

- Switch to mail: `Ctrl-1`

- Switch to calendar: `Ctrl-2`

- Move item to folder: `Ctrl+Shift+V`

- Go to folder: `Ctrl+Y`

- next message: `Ctrl+.`

- last message: `Ctrl+,`

- Find a message or other item: `CTRL+E`

- Use Advanced Find: `CTRL+SHIFT+F`
